How to play

Objective

Wipe out the enemy colony, or hold more cells by round 12.

Your turn

  • Place 4 cells per turn.
  • After your 4th, the board evolves.
  • Skip works once per game.

Territory access

①Home onlyalways available
②+ Neutralneed cells in your home zone
③+ Enemy zoneneed cells in neutral and a live cell already in enemy territory

Conway's rules

💀 Loneliness

< 2 neighbors → dies

💀 Overcrowding

> 3 neighbors → dies

✅ Survival

2–3 neighbors → lives

🟢 Birth

Empty + 3 neighbors → born in majority color

Bomb

  • Destroys a 3×3 area.
  • Unlocks at round 7 (mid-game).
  • Using it ends your turn.
  • 3-turn cooldown.

Tips

  • Groups of 2–3 survive best.
  • Reach neutral early.
  • Save your skip for a key moment.
